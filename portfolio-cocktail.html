Cocktail Connections

You awaken alone at the helm of a deep-sea submarine, the displays show you being thousand of meters below the sea. No one else seems to be in the ship except a massive anglerfish that has fused itself into the hull, demanding to be fed. Fail to do so, and it sends its spawn to tear you apart. Feed it, and it grows, straining the sub to its limits. As pressure mounts and systems fail, you must manage the chaos, repair damage, and survive the ascent in a vessel that’s slowly turning against you.
