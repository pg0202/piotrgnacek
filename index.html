About Me

Hi my name is Piotr Gnacek. Im a game designer that is based in the United Kingdom that specialises with using Unreal Engine. I'm very passionate in creating memorable experiences in video games for players to play and enjoy. My goal with each game I design is to create a unique experience for players to remember by blending engaging mechanics, immersive storytelling, and creative visuals. I’m constantly pushing to learn and grow as a designer and always aiming to craft games that leave a lasting impact.
