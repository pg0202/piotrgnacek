Puntball

Puntball is a competitive split-screen basketball first person shooter. Up to 4 players assigned to 2 teams work together to compete inside of arenas against each other to hit a ball into their opponent's goal. Each player is equipped with the "Punt Gun" that allows them to throw the ball forward, push their enemies out of their way and propel themselves using the weapons explosive knockback. Whichever teams scores the most points is the winner.
